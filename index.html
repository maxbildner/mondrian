Return On Equity Visualizer

A visualization of ROE (Return on Equity) for publicly listed US firms, inspired by Piet Mondrian paintings.

This painting is a proportional area chart. Each rectangle corresponds to a financial ratio.
The larger a rectangle's area, the larger the impact of that ratio on ROE.
